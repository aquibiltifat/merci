About Us

Empowering communities through education and welfare since 2010

Our Journey

Building Dreams, Transforming Lives

MERCI Educational and Welfare Foundation was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to create equal opportunities for underprivileged communities through education, skill development, and comprehensive welfare support.

Founded in 2010 by a group of passionate educators and social workers, we started with a single learning center in Mumbai. What began as a modest initiative to provide free tuition to 20 children has now grown into a comprehensive organization touching thousands of lives annually.

Our holistic approach combines immediate relief through ration distribution with long-term empowerment through education and skill-building programs. We believe that sustainable change comes from addressing both immediate needs and creating pathways for future success.

Our Mission

To empower underprivileged communities by providing access to quality education, vocational training, and essential welfare services, enabling them to break the cycle of poverty and build sustainable futures.

  • Provide free quality education to underprivileged children
  • Offer skill development and vocational training programs
  • Ensure food security through regular ration distribution
  • Create employment opportunities through career guidance
  • Foster community development and social welfare

Our Vision

To create an equitable society where every individual, regardless of their socio-economic background, has access to quality education, dignified livelihood opportunities, and the resources needed to thrive and contribute meaningfully to society.

  • Achieve 100% literacy in our operational communities
  • Expand our reach to underserved regions across India
  • Create sustainable employment for 10,000+ youth by 2030
  • Establish 50+ learning centers nationwide
  • Build strategic partnerships for greater impact

Our Core Values

The principles that guide every decision we make and every action we take

We approach every individual with empathy, understanding, and genuine care. We believe in treating everyone with dignity and respect, recognizing their unique challenges and potential.

We operate with complete transparency and accountability in all our activities. Every donation and resource is utilized responsibly to maximize impact for our beneficiaries.

We believe in the power of collective action and building strong, supportive communities. Together, we can create lasting change that benefits everyone.

What We Do

Our Journey Through The Years

Key milestones that shaped MERCI Foundation

2010

Foundation Established

MERCI Foundation was officially registered as a Section 8 company. Started with a single learning center serving 20 underprivileged children in Mumbai.

2012

First Ration Drive

Launched our first community welfare initiative, providing monthly rations to 25 families affected by economic challenges.

2015

Expansion & Growth

Expanded to three learning centers, reached 200+ students, and introduced vocational training programs for youth.

2018

Career Placement Program

Launched career guidance and placement services. Successfully placed 100+ students in reputed organizations within the first year.

2020

COVID-19 Response

Distributed emergency ration kits to 500+ families during the pandemic. Introduced online learning programs to ensure education continuity.

2023

Scholarship Initiative

Launched comprehensive scholarship programs supporting meritorious and economically disadvantaged students with financial aid up to ₹75,000.

2025

Digital Transformation

Implementing AI-powered learning tools and expanding our digital footprint to reach remote communities. Planning nationwide expansion.
