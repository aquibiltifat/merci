Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our programs and services

How can I volunteer with MERCI Foundation?

You can volunteer by filling out the contact form above with the subject "Volunteer Opportunity" or by emailing us directly at volunteer@mercyfoundation.org. We welcome volunteers for teaching, event management, and administrative support.

How do I apply for a scholarship?

Visit our Scholarships page and fill out the online application form. Make sure to upload all required documents including marksheets, income certificate, and ID proof. Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis.

Are donations tax-deductible?

Yes, all donations to MERCI Foundation are eligible for tax deduction under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act. We will provide you with a donation receipt for tax purposes.

Can I sponsor a specific program or child?

Yes, we offer program-specific sponsorship and child sponsorship options. Contact us through the form above or call us directly to discuss sponsorship opportunities that align with your interests.

What areas does MERCI Foundation operate in?

We primarily operate in Kanpur and surrounding areas in UttarPradesh. However, we are expanding our reach to other states and welcome partnerships to extend our services nationwide.
